About

The Learning Innovation Challenge is hackathon about education in the public service. Out of the three challenges posed to us - understanding what Learners need or want, learning as an “omni-channel” experience and extending beyond the learning programme - we chose to target the first and the third. After brainstorming, we decided to come up with an improved version of the current civil service website. We created a working prototype and prepared our pitch within 48 hours.
